# Practical Tips & Tricks

-   ğŸ› ï¸ **Use specialized tools**: For organizing information from documents, **NotebookLM** is excellent. Keep each notebook focused on a specific topic.
-   ğŸ’¡ **Good Prompting**: Give the AI a role. For example: *"You are an experienced writing professor. Please critique this essay..."*
-   ğŸ¯ **Keep it Focused**: Don't let conversations go on for too long. LLMs have limited "memory" (context windows). Start a new chat for a new topic.
-   âœ… **Plan, Revise, Execute**: For complex tasks, ask the AI to create a plan first. You review and revise the plan. Then, tell the AI to execute your revised plan.
